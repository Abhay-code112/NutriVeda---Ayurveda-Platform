Understanding the Three Doshas

Vata

Air + Space

Characteristics:
  • Thin, light build
  • Quick, creative mind
  • Variable appetite
  • Light sleep
  • Cold hands and feet
Functions:
  • Movement & circulation
  • Nervous system
  • Breathing
  • Elimination
Pitta

Fire + Water

Characteristics:
  • Medium build
  • Sharp intellect
  • Strong appetite
  • Good sleep
  • Warm body temperature
Functions:
  • Digestion & metabolism
  • Body temperature
  • Skin health
  • Vision
Kapha

Earth + Water

Characteristics:
  • Sturdy, heavy build
  • Calm, steady mind
  • Slow digestion
  • Deep sleep
  • Cool, moist skin
Functions:
  • Structure & immunity
  • Lubrication
  • Emotional stability
  • Joint health

Dosha Balance Recommendations

Dietary Guidelines
For Vata Imbalance:

Warm, cooked foods; sweet, sour, salty tastes; regular meal times

For Pitta Imbalance:

Cool, fresh foods; sweet, bitter, astringent tastes; avoid spicy foods

For Kapha Imbalance:

Light, warm foods; pungent, bitter, astringent tastes; avoid heavy foods

Lifestyle Guidelines
For Vata:

Regular routine, gentle exercise, meditation, warm environment

For Pitta:

Moderate exercise, cool environment, avoid overwork, practice patience

For Kapha:

Vigorous exercise, stimulating activities, warm environment, avoid oversleep
