Understanding Ayurvedic Prakriti

The Three Doshas:

  • Vata (Air + Space): Movement, circulation, nervous system
  • Pitta (Fire + Water): Metabolism, digestion, transformation
  • Kapha (Earth + Water): Structure, immunity, lubrication

Prakriti is your natural constitution determined at conception. Understanding your Prakriti helps in:

  • Choosing appropriate diet and lifestyle
  • Preventing disease through proper balance
  • Optimizing physical and mental well-being
